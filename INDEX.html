Brazil is an attractive country to visit for many reasons, including

Its nature

Brazil is one of the best places in the world for ecotourism, with jungles, dunes and beaches.

Its gastronomy

Brazilian cuisine is a mix of cultures that includes feijoada, rodizio meats, fish and seafood soups, and street empanadas.

Your carnival

The Brazilian Carnival is one of the most famous in the world and is celebrated at the end of February.

Their cities

Brazil has historic cities, such as Salvador de Bahía, and metropolitan cities, such as São Paulo, where you can visit museums, monuments and attend cultural festivals.

Its attractions

Among the best-known attractions in Brazil are the Statue of Christ the Redeemer in Rio de Janeiro and the Iguazu Falls.
